COMP126: Practical Web Design & Development for Everyone

Course Schedule, Spring 2022

go to syllabus & policies

important notes

  • This schedule is subject to change based on your needs and interests and other, less predictable events. This page is the only up-to-date version.
  • Week 1 classes will be fully remote on Zoom, at https://unc.zoom.us/j/99561731410
  • Starting in Week 3, unless you hear otherwise from the professor, in-person classes will be held in SN 014 with option to join via Zoom. See the Course Format section of the Syllabus & Policies page for details.
  • Readings/viewings/setup tasks should be completed before class on the days on which they're listed.
  • Quizzes will take place at the start of class time and will last 30 minutes, unless you have extended time accommodations.
